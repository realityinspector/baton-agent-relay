# Borealis Brand Guide (v3, 2026)

Borealis helps self-employed people understand their cash flow without a CFO.

Core promise: **clarity without condescension.**

Audience: freelancers, indie consultants, small studio owners. Mostly solo operators
running businesses they don't have an MBA for.

Three pillars (apply in this order when in doubt):
1. **Specific, not vague.** Numbers and timeframes, not "soon."
2. **Confident, not cocky.** We know taxes; we don't gloat.
3. **Boring where it matters.** Security, accuracy, money totals — never cute.
